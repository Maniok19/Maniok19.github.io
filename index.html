Key Features

Trick of the Day

Discover new skateboarding tricks every day with our curated "Trick of the Day" feature. Each trick includes detailed descriptions, difficulty levels, and video tutorials to help you master new moves.

Community Forum

Join discussions with fellow skaters, share your experiences, ask questions, and get advice. Our forum creates a supportive environment for skaters of all skill levels to connect and learn from each other.

Skate Spots Discovery

Find and share skateboarding locations worldwide. Discover new spots, read reviews, and contribute to the community by adding your favorite skating locations with detailed descriptions and photos.

About WikiTricks

The Story Behind WikiTricks

WikiTricks was born from my passion for skateboarding. As a skater myself, I noticed that information about tricks and skating locations was often hard to find.

The inspiration came during a skating session where I was trying to learn a new trick but couldn't find comprehensive resources. I wanted a website like Wikipedia with the skateboarding community - WikiTricks was born.

This project represents 2 months of development, combining my love for skateboarding with my passion for web development. It's a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, where I've been able to apply full-stack development skills to create something for the skateboarding community.
